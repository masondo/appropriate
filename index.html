Innovation

The ETCH Clock is a revolution in timetelling design. The elastic face bends itself to engrave the sleek numbers you see displayed, then returns to its initial form. It is a result of many years of hard work from the people at 42foundry.

Technical Information

  • 40 x 40 cm
  • approximately 6 grams
  • power supply of approximately 10 watts
  • aluminum framing
